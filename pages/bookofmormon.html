Introduction

The Introduction page of the Book of Mormon is a great page to share with people as an invitation.

Instead of handing out copies of the Book of Mormon, consider handing out just the introduction page as something to read before the whole book is introduced.


Language Learning Audio

The Book of Mormon Audio files can be a useful resource for learning a language. An effective method of learning pronounciation is by listening to the files while reading a copy of the Book of Mormon in that language here.
