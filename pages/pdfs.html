Member Missionary Work and Finding the Elect of God

Brother Scott Marshall, at age 26, left a fiancee, a house, and a successful Corvette restoration business to serve a mission.

This book is a collection of the best missionary practices he discovered in over thirty years. It is completely compatible with Preach My Gospel, and has been used to train missionaries in over 150 missions. Over 80,000 copies have been distributed.

Book of Mormon on Trial

The Book of Mormon on Trial is the work of Jack West, a Stanford Law student who had to defend a subject for a final exam. He decided to defend the Book of Mormon as if it were on trial in a United States Court room. The result was put in a comic book, and helps investigators become interested in the reading the Book of Mormon

This book has been used for investigators who have trouble reading boring old books.

It can be found on Amazon or here.
