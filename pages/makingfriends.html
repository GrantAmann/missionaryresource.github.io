How to Make Friends

The Gospel of Jesus Christ is all about making friends! Here are some tips to help.


Finding Things in Common

Many times your local missionaries will have copies of the Book of Mormon that they will give out to friends. Ask your missionaries for a spare copy to hand out to a friend of your own.
