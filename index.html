Missionary Resource

This website is a resource for all things related to missionary work for the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints. It is run by Grant Amann, a member from Salt Lake City. Grant served a mission in Mendoza, Argentina, where he fell in love with the gospel of Jesus Christ. This page is meant to be a quick easy resource for missionary efforts. The views and values expressed do not represent the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints. This is not an official Church website.

If you have any Word Documents, PDFs, Videos, or MP3s you would like to add to this website, please email me at gamann@usc.edu
